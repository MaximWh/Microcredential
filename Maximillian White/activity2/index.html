Why should you visit?
If you read for fun or just to learn new things, New York City's Public Libraries are the place for you. Here you will have a wide selection of books to choose from. As an added bonus it is free to come in so don't hesitate to stop and grab a book.
Convenience
You will never have to worry about traveling far out of your way to get to a library. We have many branches placed all around NYC to make it easier for you to come see us.
